#mechanic 
As the player progresses and places certain buildings within the world, the spawning of the world will change in a way that is called natural evolution. 

When a player first upgrades a nest to a new tier, it triggers that tier of natural evolution. i.e. when the first nest is upgraded to tier three, that means that tier three nests can now spawn in the wild. Nests are capped at the highest available natural evolution tier and can not go higher. Nests can upgrade naturally and will over time. 

Natural evolution also caps out at level 3, meaning that upgrading a nest to level 4 does increase natural evolution.
