The critter network covers one entire surface at a time. When there are separate sections of network that are not physically connected, their contents are still made available to the network at large. Structures like the [[pheromone tower]] extend the network, as well as [[template nest]]s and [[freight stations]]. These extensions are applied in a similar manner to lightning collectors on Fulgora, creating a round, fluid network that grows alongside the factory. In order for critters to access the inventories of critter logistics systems, those systems must be placed under the active critter network.
