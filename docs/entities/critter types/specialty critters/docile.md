Docile [[critter]]s only spawn from [[docile nest]]s. Docile nests spawn naturally within the world. When a docile nest becomes a hostile nests, a number of critters equal to that nests critter limit also become hostile. The same applies to docile nests becoming tame, as well as the tiers of nests increasing.
