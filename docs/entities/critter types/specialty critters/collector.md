#critter #entity 
A collector is a special type of [[critter]] that takes items from the ground. The items need to be within the biter network. They take these items and deposit them in [[biter depot]]s. They have a small bonus to speed, and a great bonus to carrying capacity. Collectors are replaced by [[logistic]] critters at nest tier 3 and above.

Tier 1
Nest: tier 1
Able to move things from ground to depos. More carrying capacity compared to any bitter in this tier. Long downtime between tasks.
Stats: +1 haul capacity (2), +%5 speed, -5% [[critter idle chance]] -10% [[chitin yield]]

Tier 2
Nest: tier 2
Able to move things from ground to depos. Increase carrying capacity. Less downtime between tasks. Slight speed boost.
Stats: +3 haul capacity (4), +10% speed, -10% [[critter idle chance]], -20% [[chitin yield]]
