All nests will have two stats: tier and type. The type of nest determines the type of biter that nest spawns on spawn trigger, and the tier of the nest determines the stats of the biter it spawns. 

Nest levels provide stat multipliers to the type of biter they spawn. 

Tier 0: nest spawns small biters/spitters only, with no stat modifiers. 

Tier 1: small biters/spitters, +10% stat

Tier 2: medium biters/spitters, +20% stat

Tier 3: big biters/spitters, +30% stat

Tier 4: behemoth biters/spitters, +50% stat

Tier 5: behemoth biters/spitters, +75% stat (also highly specialized)
